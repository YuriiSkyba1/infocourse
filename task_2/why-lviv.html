In addition to its historical heritage, cultural and touristic importance, the western city of Lviv has been showing steady growth in the past few years, becoming one of the major tech hubs in the country, alongside Kyiv and Kharkiv.

Lviv is home to 492 tech companies and HQs of global software providers. According to the Global Outsourcing 100 2019, 8 out of 17 Ukrainian tech companies included in the top shortlist, chose Lviv to have offices or headquarters.

As an attractive investment destination, the tech market in Lviv is economically feasible for starting a tech business. The city boasts qualified human capital and offers many opportunities for potential companies due to the high-quality services, which make Lviv one of the most competitive European tech hubs.

Lviv IT Cluster: driving change through community

In 2011, a community of the leading tech companies, municipal authorities, and educational institutions formed the Lviv IT Cluster, which aims to improve the local economy and provide sustainable income to the city. The initiative was set up within the Lviv Competitiveness Strategy, and since its launch, continues to implement large-scale projects that contribute to positioning Lviv as one of the attractive tech hubs both in Ukraine and CEE. Lviv IT Cluster unites over 100 member companies, 18,000 tech professionals, and implements projects in such areas as promotion, education, development of international relations, and infrastructure.
